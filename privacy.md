# Privacy Policy for OpenClaw Discord Bot

Last updated: 2026-04-08

This Privacy Policy describes how OpenClaw Team ("we", "us", "our") processes data when you use the OpenClaw Discord Bot (the "Bot").

## 1. Data we process

Depending on how you use the Bot, we may process:

- Discord user identifiers, server identifiers, channel identifiers, and message identifiers,
- message content sent to the Bot,
- basic technical and operational metadata (timestamps, request/response status, error logs),
- configuration and telemetry needed to run, monitor, and secure the Bot.

## 2. How we use data

We process data to:

- provide Bot functionality and respond to user requests,
- maintain reliability, performance, and security,
- troubleshoot issues and prevent abuse.

## 3. Legal basis and sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We may share limited data with infrastructure or service providers strictly as needed to operate the Bot (for example hosting, logging, or model providers), subject to their terms and security controls.

## 4. Retention

We retain data only as long as reasonably necessary for service operation, security, troubleshooting, and legal compliance.

## 5. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 6. Your rights

You may request access to or deletion of your data, subject to legal and operational requirements.

## 7. Children's privacy

The Bot is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law and Discord policy.

## 8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after updates means you accept the revised policy.
